# Visual Identity — Victory Fit (New Logo)

Colors extracted from the current logo files (`logo-version-a-full.png` and `logo-version-b-icon.png`).

## Logo Colors

| Name             | Hex       | RGB              | Usage                                      |
|------------------|-----------|------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Burgundy Red     | `#7A1B1F` | rgb(122,27,31)   | Primary — VF letters, dominant logo color   |
| Orange           | `#F29031` | rgb(242,144,49)  | Accent — weight bars, heartbeat line        |
| White            | `#FEFEFE` | rgb(254,254,254) | Text — "VICTORY FIT", "SPORTIVEMENT METZ"  |

## Notes

- The **Burgundy Red** (`#7A1B1F`) is significantly darker than the original charte graphique red (`#E53534`). This is the new dominant brand color.
- The **Orange** (`#F29031`) is very close to the original charte graphique orange (`#F6A13B`), slightly more saturated.
- The heartbeat line uses a gradient from orange to a deeper orange-red (`#EF6C3E`).
- The original charte graphique Gold Yellow (`#FAC564`) is no longer present in the logo.
